About Us

CREDIFY is a student-led innovation aiming to bring structure and transparency to the influencer marketing space.

We believe creators should be recognized not just by their follower count, but by real impact. Brands should have access to reliable tools that help them make smarter collaboration decisions.

Our Mission

  • Empower genuine creators with fair scoring and public profiles.
  • Help brands identify the right influencers based on real performance data.
  • Bridge the trust gap between creators and brands.
